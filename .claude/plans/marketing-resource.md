## Core Value Propositions

### 1. GBP-to-Website in Minutes
**Feature:** One-click Google Business Profile import populates business name, categories, services, location, phone, and reviews — then AI generates an entire website.
**Benefit:** Business owners go from zero to a published, professional website without writing a single word or touching a line of code.
**Proof Point:** Sites include home page, about page, contact page, individual service pages, service area pages, neighborhood pages, and FAQ hub — all generated automatically.

### 2. Every Page is Unique — No Template Copy
**Feature:** AI generates unique content for every service page, service area, neighborhood, and category — informed by the business's actual services, location, credentials, and local context.
**Benefit:** No duplicate content penalties from Google. Each page targets different search terms, multiplying the business's chances of ranking.
**Proof Point:** Neighborhood pages include real local landmarks, schools, housing types, and community details. Service area pages reference actual cities and regions. Service pages include problems solved, detailed how-it-works sections, and FAQs.

### 3. Google Search Console Feedback Loop
**Feature:** Connects to Google Search Console to pull real search query data (what people actually search to find the site), then feeds those terms back into the AI content generation prompts.
**Benefit:** Content continuously improves based on real customer search behavior — not guesswork. The site gets smarter over time.
**How It Works:**
1. User connects their Google Search Console property
2. System syncs search queries (clicks, impressions, CTR, average position)
3. Dashboard shows top queries and content opportunities (high-impression, low-ranking terms)
4. On next content regeneration, AI uses these real search terms to naturally optimize page content
5. Better content → better rankings → more search data → repeat

### 4. Hyper-Local SEO at Scale
**Feature:** Generates dedicated pages for every service area (city) and neighborhood the business serves, each with unique localized content.
**Benefit:** Captures "near me" and "[service] in [city]" searches — the highest-intent local searches that convert to calls.
**Details:**
- Service area pages for each city served
- Neighborhood pages with local landmarks, schools, housing character, and community info
- AI uses latitude/longitude data to reference real local features
- Internal linking between neighborhoods, service areas, and services creates a strong site structure

### 5. Built-In SEO Best Practices
**Feature:** Every page automatically includes structured data (JSON-LD schema markup), optimized meta titles/descriptions, proper heading hierarchy, breadcrumb navigation, and internal linking.
**Benefit:** The site is technically optimized for Google from day one — no SEO consultant needed.
**Schema Types:** LocalBusiness, Service, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList, WebSite, Organization — all validated with zero errors.

### 6. Google Reviews Integration
**Feature:** Pulls real Google reviews from the business's Google Business Profile and displays them on the website with star ratings.
**Benefit:** Social proof is built into the site automatically — builds trust with visitors and improves conversion rates.
**Details:** Average rating and review count displayed in hero section and trust bar. Individual reviews shown in testimonials section.

### 7. Client Settings That Shape Every Page
**Feature:** Business owners customize voice & tone, target audience, "about the business" description, credentials, local details, and specific content requests — all of which feed into every AI generation prompt.
**Benefit:** The generated content sounds like the business, not like a robot. It mentions their license numbers, years of experience, service specialties, and local knowledge.
**Settings Include:**
- Voice & Tone (Professional, Friendly, Authoritative, etc.)
- Point of View (We/Our, I/My, etc.)
- Words to always use / never use
- Target audience description
- Business credentials & awards
- Local details (housing stock, climate, community context)
- Specific content requests

### 8. Real-Time Build Progress
**Feature:** When content is generating, the dashboard shows live progress with percentage complete, step-by-step status, and build logs.
**Benefit:** Business owners can watch their site being built in real time — no black box, no wondering "is it working?"
**Details:** Inngest-powered pipeline with step functions. Progress updates every 3 seconds. Shows which services, areas, and pages are being generated.

### 9. Instant Updates After Settings Changes
**Feature:** When business owners update their services, service areas, neighborhoods, branding, or any settings, the system immediately revalidates all affected pages.
**Benefit:** Changes go live instantly — no waiting for caches to expire or manual deployments.

### 10. Multi-Category Service Organization
**Feature:** Services are organized under GBP categories (primary and secondary), creating a logical site structure with category landing pages and nested service pages.
**Benefit:** Clean URL structure and site architecture that Google understands — services grouped logically like a real business organizes them.
**Example:** A plumber might have categories like "Plumbing" (primary) with services like "Water Heater Repair" and "Drain Cleaning", plus "HVAC" (secondary) with "AC Installation" and "Furnace Repair."

## Messaging Guidelines

### Do Say:
- "AI-powered" (not "AI-generated" — implies quality)
- "SEO-optimized" (specific, measurable)
- "Unique content for every page" (differentiator)
- "Built from your Google Business Profile" (easy onboarding story)
- "Gets smarter over time" (Search Console feedback loop)
- "No coding required"
- "Professional website in minutes"

### Don't Say:
- "Automated" (sounds impersonal)
- "Template" (even though we use templates — the content is unique)
- "Cheap" (say "affordable" or "fraction of the cost")
- "Replace your SEO agency" (too aggressive — say "SEO built in")
- "AI writes your website" (say "AI generates unique content tailored to your business")
